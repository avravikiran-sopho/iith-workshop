How to use?

  • To submit a work order click on 'Submit Order' button on top and fill the details.
  • After submitting the form, a mail will be sent to the refernce professor (Approver 1) who can either approve or reject it.
  • A link will be sent to the student for tracking status of work order.
  • Workshop Staff (Approver 2) and Workshop Faculty team (Approver 3) can only approve orders when their preceeding approvers approve it.
  • Approver2 and Approver3 should login to approve orders.Login is not required for submiiting orders and reference professor.
  • Work order will be discarded if any one of the approver rejects it and the work order will not be forwarded to subsequent approvers.
  • List of all orders can be seen by clicking on 'All orders' on the top menu.
  • When all 3 approvers approves the order, Workshop staff (Approver 2) can update status regarding workorder till completion.
  • Any update regarding the workorder will be informed to the student through mail.
